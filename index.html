2D Bullet Hell Game

Step into the fast-paced, heart-pounding action of a 2D Bullet Hell game, where sharp reflexes and precision are your keys to success. Your mission is to focus on the orange circle, your primary target, to boost your score while dodging a relentless barrage of bullets. With each hit, your score climbs, challenging you to achieve the highest score possible. Featuring chaotic bullet patterns, tight controls, and an exhilarating gameplay loop, this game delivers nonstop action for players who thrive on precision and quick thinking.

3D Survival Game

In this collaborative project, my primary focus was on procedural generation and optimization to enhance both gameplay and performance. I developed the system responsible for procedurally generating the terrain and desert buildings, creating a dynamic and immersive environment. Additionally, I implemented procedural generation for the interior layouts of buildings, ensuring varied and engaging exploration experiences for players. To support smooth gameplay, I worked on optimizing the terrain rendering, significantly reducing performance bottlenecks and delivering a seamless and visually appealing experience.

Unity Engine Projects

2D GAME

In this 2D game centered around ocean conservation, players embark on multiple quests aimed at cleaning the sea and saving marine life. As part of a collaborative team effort, I contributed significantly to the design and development of the first two quests, laying the groundwork for the game's narrative and environmental goals. Additionally, I worked on enhancing the game's atmosphere by implementing environmental effects, including a dynamic parallax background to create a sense of depth, realistic wave animations, and immersive sound design to bring the underwater world to life. I also developed the mechanic where the water darkens as players swim deeper, intensifying the experience and adding a layer of immersion.

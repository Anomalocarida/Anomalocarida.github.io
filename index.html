3D Survival Game (Team Project)

A 3D Survival Game where you have to find and traverse buildings to obtain food and water in order to survive.


This is a team project where i mainly worked on the procedural generation of the terrain and buildings in the desert, as well as the procedural generation of the interior layout of buildings. I also worked on optimizing the rendering of the terrain in order to create smooth gameplay with little performance issues.
